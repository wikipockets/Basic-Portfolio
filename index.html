About Me

Stephen Johnson currently works in the Commercial A/V field. He has a passion for creating art with lighting effects with a geometric feel blended with a touch of natural order. A charlotte native, born amidst the throng of charlotte's banking boom. He grew up watching the city change around him from a fair city to a thriving metropolis.

Finding an interest in art when he was young. He grew up in an environment that encouraged free creation influenced by imagination. In school he began to play low in band and continued on that path until graduation. Also was fond of technology, growing up in the digital age has allowed him to find an understanding of technology not found in generations previous.
